The Best Christmas Movies According to Rigo

My top 3 christmas movies of all-time.

The Christmas Chronicles (2018)

In this Netflix original, Kurt Russell is an uncommonly cool, handsome and wise-talking Santa Claus, who helps a brother and sister (Judah Lewis and Darby Camp) rediscover the magic of Christmas.

The Holiday Calendar (2018)

Kat Graham, Quincy Brown and Ethan Peck star in this romantic drama about a photographer whose newly inherited antique advent calendar may predict the future.

The Knight Before Christmas (2019)

In this gleefully cheesy and admirably bonkers rom-com, a modern-day 30-something (Vanessa Hudgens) falls for an English knight (Josh Whitehouse) from the 14th century.
